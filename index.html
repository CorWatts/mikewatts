I'm a musician. More specifically, I'm an orchestrator, pianist, synthesist, composer, arranger, and on occasion I even play accordion. The majority of my career has centered around music for film and television, and that's kept me busy for thirty (plus) years. In the course of that time I've worked with many very gifted people, which in turn has regularly (way too regularly) reintroduced me to my own humility. The other side of the coin is I also enjoy a little teaching at both Biola University as well as Azuza Pacific University. Both are very fine schools.

It's been my privilege to have been (and continue to be) involved with many wonderful films over the years.

One of the first films I worked on was Unforgiven, composed by Lennie Niehaus and directed by Clint Eastwood. Since then I've been quite fortunate to have worked with many of the most gifted composers/artists in the industry. They would include John Williams, James Horner, Alan Silvestri, John Debney, Michael Giacchino, James Newton Howard, Lalo Schifrin, Bruce Broughton, Randy Newman, Christophe Beck, Alan Menken, and so many others.

It's also been my pleasure over the years to have been involved with music for the Disney parks. From Fireworks shows, projection shows (such as The World of Color), to music for rides (such as The Haunted Mansion and Cars), live music (such as The Disneyland Band), and live theatre (such as Frozen Live at The Hyperion), the creative talent behind the scenes at the Disney parks is quite impressive. It's always a pleasure for me to be involved with them!
